Record

Choose the record mode

After calling the bot, please switch to the message window. Please click the record button on the message card.

Type in the username

Please type in the username for your voice.  The username is case-insensitive.

Record

Record the pre-defined sentences following the instruction of the bot. You are welcomed to customise the sentences yourself. The whole recording takes around 10 mins. After the recording, please do not hang up until the bot hang up.

Call

Choose the call mode

After calling the bot, please switch to the message window. Please click the call button on the message card.

Type in the username

Please type in the username you would like to call. The username is case-insensitive. If the username does not existed, you will have a conversation with a bot voice. You can try a chat with our developer's voice under the  username "ShiminAng" or "Xuenan".
